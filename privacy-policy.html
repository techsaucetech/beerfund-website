Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and transaction data, as well as anonymous usage data when you interact with our application.

2. Use of Financial Information via Plaid

Our app uses Plaid Inc. ("Plaid") to connect to your financial institution. By using our service, you grant us and Plaid the right to access and transmit your financial information from your connected account(s). This access is read-only and used solely to enable features such as transaction tracking and budgeting tools.

For information on how Plaid handles your data, please refer to the Plaid End User Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • • Provide and maintain the application
  • • Personalize user experience
  • • Improve functionality and performance
  • • Ensure security and fraud prevention
  • • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information may be shared with service providers solely for the purpose of supporting our app functionality.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights under applicable privacy laws, such as the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with the new effective date.
